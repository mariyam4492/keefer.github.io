Department of IT and CS

Department of IT and Computer Science at PAF-IAST is the largest academic department. We offer admissions in four undergraduate programs namely: Computer Science, Software Engineering, Artificial Intelligence, and Data Science, and one graduate program i.e., Data Science. Degree programs are designed with concept of international exposure that can equip the students to high-tech customized systems. Our Austria-trained (Fachhochschule certified), Ph.D. faculty in diverse computing fields is committed to prepare students for national and international service by raising their skills to secure the required placements around the globe.

Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering

The Department of Electrical & Computer Engineering (E&CE) has been established in 2020 under the exclusive vision “Skilling Pakistan” of the PAF-IAST. E&CE contributes to expansion and enhancement of the quality engineering education in electrical, computer, and allied disciplines and simultaneously to stimulate related high-technology industry in Pakistan. E&CE strives to impart the highest quality engineering education in Pakistan that matches with that provided in developed countries. E&CE produces professionals equipped with engineering knowledge, management, and technical skills such as necessary mathematical tools, scientific basics to serve in different sectors such as communications, computers, robotics, artificial intelligence, embedded systems, internet of things, energy and power systems, web development, software development, automation, and electronics in national and international markets. The PAF-IAST has collaboration for Bachelor and Master programs with Austrian and Chinese universities; thus, curricula of the degree programmes offered are prepared at par with the international standard that have courses and hands-on experience required for the cutting-edge research and product development.

Department of Chemical and Energy Engineering

Guided by foreign qualified faculty with engineering work experiences from industry-leading companies such as Paki American Fertilizer, Marri Petroleum Company, Lucky Cement, Rafhan Maize, and doctorates from Imperial College London (UK), INHA University (Korea), Universiti Teknologi Malaysia, Wuhan University of Technology (China) and Otto Von Guericke University (Germany), chemical engineering students benefit from a truly exceptional experience. The department faculty makes sure that students obtain the skills and preparation that are needed to be successful in their future professional and academic careers. Students will typically have multiple work experiences through professional internships or Co-ops before they graduate. In addition, students are exposed to numerous plant trips, professional and academic speakers, and have research mentors.

Department of Biomedical Sciences

The field of Biomedical Sciences encompasses both biology & medicine to solve human health issues. PAF-IAST, Haripur is the first institute in KP to establish Department of Biomedical Sciences. The teaching and research at Department focus on various aspects of medical sciences, ranging from fundamental understanding of molecular basis of cells & their interactions, to highly advanced area of knowledge related to comprehension of human pathologies. Our institute is first in the country to offer a dual degree in the field of biomedical sciences in collaboration with Austrian Institutions. Foremost, the Department aims to deliver students with an exceptional and individualized educational experience to enable them to skillfully solve biomedical science related problems.
